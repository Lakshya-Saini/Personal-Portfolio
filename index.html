Ambith Motors

Automobile vehicle service provider. Customer can book an home, office or on road appointment for car service and can sell their car as well as can buy a new car with this platform. The project includes a website and multiple dashboards for different users.

Working Functionalities -

The basic project functionality is that it allows customers to book a car service wherever they are whether at home, office or on road. They can book an appointment and the nearest technician will receive a notification about the service. Technician can confirm or decline the service depending upon his availability. If confirmed, he will receive customer's coordinates on map with route and total distance. After car is serviced, technicians can change the service status from their app and with that customer will recieve a confirmation and payment notification which then lead to payment gateway.

In addition to this, this app also allows customers to sell their old car and can buy a new one.

Working Functionalities -

Similar to Netflix, it provides different sections and categories for movies and TV Series. By clicking on any of these we can see their details and similar items. It also includes a watch now button to see the trailer. It is built in mern stack with passport auth and data is fetched from TMDB API.

It is an ongoing project. Features like movie streaming, account accessibility limitations, manage profile, movie and tv series recommendations, recently viewed, and even more to be added in upcoming versions.
